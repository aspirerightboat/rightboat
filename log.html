General Description

'Rayndeer', Wauquiez 55 Pilot Saloon, hull no. 4, built in 2008 in France by Wauquiez to a Berret / Racoupeau design with Couedel Hugon interior. White GRP/Balsa sandwich hull with dark blue hull lines, superstructure with teak laid deck, round bilged hull with lead fin with bulb keel. Twin wheel steering position. Fuel tank: 600lt; freshwater tank: 850lt; wastewater tanks (2): 170lt.
